Yi Lantang Exhibition

History and Culture Exhibition of Yilantang Scenic Area.

The historical and cultural exhibition of Yilantang Scenic Area clearly shows the 200-year-old historical context and rich heritage of Yilantang Scenic Area. Using modern two-dimensional computer animation and post-synthesis technology to produce "Ice Play Classic" animation short film, supplemented by traditional multimedia landscape video short film, unique ancient architectural models of the scenic spot, etc., display various forms, rich content, and full of fun. The historical and cultural exhibition of Yilantang Scenic Area has created a visual and auditory feast in all aspects.
